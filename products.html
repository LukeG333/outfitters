What Every Product Includes

The same pledge on everything, from small accessories to big investments.

  • Your gear includes a material breakdown detailing its components and where they were collected.
  • A commitment to our lifetime repair service: we fix it, not replace it.
  • A donation to coastline cleanup is incorporated into the price of every item.
  • Hardware and finishes are durable, built for the harsh New England marine environment.
  • Packaging is plastic-free and made from recycled cardboard.
  • If you're not satisfied within 60 days, simply send it back for a refund (details in our policies).
