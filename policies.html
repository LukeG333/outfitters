Store Policies

Clear terms, with no hidden catches.

Here's what you need to know before making a purchase, including payment, shipping, and returns.

Financing

Because great gear should be accessible, we've simplified our payment options:

  • You can pay with major credit and debit cards, as well as Apple Pay and Google Pay.
  • Split any order over $150 into four interest-free payments through Pay-in-4, billed bi-weekly.
  • Apply for a TideLine Outfitter Card for 0% APR for 12 months on purchases of $500 or more, including kayaks (subject to approval).
  • Verified commercial fishermen and marine non-profit organizations can apply for net-30 terms.
  • Massachusetts sales tax is calculated at checkout as required.

Delivery

We ship every order in eco-friendly, plastic-free recycled packaging:

  • Free standard shipping (3 to 6 business days) is included on orders over $75; for all other orders, it's a flat rate of $6.50.
  • Expedited shipping (2 business days) is available for $14.95.
  • Local pickup is available free of charge at our Scituate workshop, typically ready within 24 hours.
  • Kayaks and other large items will ship via freight; we'll contact you with a scheduling window once your order is ready.
  • We currently ship throughout the United States. Orders placed before 12:00 PM ET on a business day will be shipped on the same day.

Return Policy

Not quite the right fit? No worries.

  1. Simply return any unused item in its original condition within 60 days for a full refund to your original payment method.
  2. Start a return from your order confirmation email or by reaching out to our customer service team.
  3. We'll provide you with a prepaid, carbon-offset return shipping label. Return shipping is on us for defects and free for any reason within 30 days.
  4. Your refund will be processed within five business days of your return arriving at our workshop.
  5. Note: Custom-engraved items and final-sale items are non-returnable but are still covered by our repair program.

Lifetime Repair Promise

We encourage repairs before returns. Here's how we'll help:

  • We will repair any TideLine product for the lifetime of your ownership.
  • Repairs for typical wear and tear are free of charge. For accidental damage, we'll charge only our cost to repair.
  • Items that are beyond repair will be recycled and upcycled into new materials, never ending up in landfills.
